What we look for

There are two things we look for in applicants:

  1. You're a good team player - we spend almost all of our time working in pairs and groups, so if you prefer working alone, this isn't the right school for you.
  2. You've tried programming before, so that you know that you like it and can be successful at it.

The process

After you submit your application, we'll contact you within two weeks to set up a phone interview. After the phone interview, we'll let you know if you're accepted within two weeks. If you're accepted, you'll have one week to decide whether you'd like to enroll and make your first payment.

We consider applications on a rolling basis. There are no deadlines, but the earlier you apply, the better your chances of being accepted.

If you have any questions at all, please email us.

The programming tests

To make sure that you have tried enough programming to know that Epicodus will be a good use of your time and money, you must be able to solve the layout test and the Ping-Pong test (the example is done in JavaScript, but you're welcome to use another language if you have experience elsewhere).

If you don't know how to complete these tests yet, that's fine! Leave that part of the application blank and go through the first chapters of the Epicodus curriculum at www.learnhowtoprogram.com. When you have your solutions ready, email applications@epicodus.com.
